# capstone_project

-In this project, I worked on a dataset containing sales pieces of Northwind Traders, a fictitious specialty food export/import company. I touched on various points such as the company's earnings change, product stock numbers, supplier analysis, employees analysis, logistics company analysis, analysis on customer rfm scores.I also created a power bi dashboard and made a presentation.
